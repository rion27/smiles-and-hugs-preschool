Our Programs

Playgroup

A gentle introduction to a structured day, focusing on social skills and motor development through play.

Nursery

Building foundational literacy and numeracy skills through fun, thematic activities and storytelling.

Kindergarten (Jr. & Sr.)

Preparing for formal schooling with a curriculum that balances academics, art, and physical education.

Full Daycare

A safe and caring "home away from home" for working parents, including meals, nap time, and activities.

Art & Craft

Letting creativity flourish with painting, crafting, and sensory play to develop fine motor skills.

Music & Dance

Exploring rhythm, movement, and expression in a joyful group setting. Fun for everyone!
